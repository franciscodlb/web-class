Japanese art is known for the traditional and cultural elements it reflects. Thanks to this mixture of cultures and symbolism it has positioned itself as one of the benchmarks of world art. This Asian country seems to be home to two different worlds. On the one hand, it is known for the great technological contributions it has made to the world, but on the other hand, Japan is full of tradition and ancient cultures. The result of these two elements has resulted in very deep and interesting artistic expressions.
